Fellowship of the Services

The Fellowship of the Services is an association created out of the unforgettable experiences of the 1914 – 1918 War and all conflicts since then.

Admission to the Fellowship is that providing a man is eligible under one or more of the clauses below, his admission should not be denied due to his nationality.Â  No man shall be eligible for admission as a Companion unless:

He has served in the Royal Navy, Royal Marines, British Army, Royal Air Force or in Forces auxiliary to these.
National Servicemen shall be eligible for admission after their term of service.
Regular or National Servicemen who have joined the Territorial Forces following their term of service are eligible.
He has served in the Mercantile Marine on War Service.
