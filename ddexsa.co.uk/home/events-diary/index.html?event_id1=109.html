Derby & District Ex-Services Association

Events Diary

There are a number of events organised throughout the year within the City of Derby and the surrounding District where the Services and ex-Service personnel and there families are involved. These events range from ceremonial events to social events.

The following events will take place in Derby or Derbyshire (and in some cases the bordering Counties of Nottinghamshire and Staffordshire) during the coming months.
